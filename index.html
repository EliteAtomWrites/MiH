How Michael & Hammed Works

Our secure escrow process makes buying and selling services simple and safe

1

Buyer Places Order

Buyer selects a gig and pays through our secure system. Funds are held in escrow.

2

Seller Completes Work

Seller delivers the service according to the agreed terms and timeline.

3

Buyer Approves

Buyer reviews the work and approves completion. They can request revisions if needed.

4

Secure Payout

Funds are released from escrow to the seller, minus our small commission fee.
